About AutoIPA

AutoIPA is an AI-powered phonetic transcription tool that uses a pre-trained model to convert your recording to International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) output.

The tool currently uses the facebook/wav2vec2-xlsr-53-espeak-cv-ft model, which you can check out at Hugging Face.

Results may not be accurate and precision varies with language. The model does not separate words automatically, so it is recommended to use single words or short phrases. For better results, speak slowly and clearly.

AutoIPA is a project created and maintained by Guilherme Brizzi.

Privacy notice: AutoIPA stores your recording and its transcription to help with further development. No personal identification is collected. By using this service, you agree to such terms.
